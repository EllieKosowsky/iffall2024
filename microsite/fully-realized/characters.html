Characters of The Nutcracker

Clara Stahlbaum

The main character of the story, a young girl who goes to the Land of Sweets on Christmas Eve

Drosselmeyer

Clara's godfather, a mysterious clock and toymaker who gives Clara a wooden nutcracker for Christmas

Sugar Plum Fairy

A beautiful fairy who resembles Clara's sister Louise. In some productions, Clara transforms into the Sugar Plum Fairy at the end of the ballet

The Nutcracker Prince

A recognizable character in the ballet

The Mouse King

A character in the story

Fritz

Clara's brother

Louise

Clara's sister
