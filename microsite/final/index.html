The Nutcracker

The Nutcracker ballet follows the magical journey of a young girl named Clara, who receives a nutcracker doll on Christmas Eve. That night, her Nutcracker comes to life, battling the Mouse King and leading Clara on an enchanted journey filled with dancing snowflakes, a Sugar Plum Fairy, and a celebration of sweets from around the world.
